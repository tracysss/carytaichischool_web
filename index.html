Cary Tai Chi School offers group and private Tai Chi classes.
We focus on teaching traditional Yang-Style Tai Chi.

Our Yang Family Tai Chi Association certified instructors strive to pass on Tai Chi knowledge and skills in the most classic and authentic way.

Benefits of Tai Chi

  • Reduce stress
  • Improve balance
  • Build muscles
  • Learn self-defense skills

Yang-Style Tai Chi

  • The most popular and most accessible style of Tai Chi
  • Slow and steady
  • Emphasis on relaxation
  • Beautiful to watch

Our Offers

  • Group classes on Tai Chi Hand Forms, Sword Forms, and Saber Forms
  • Private customized classes students of all levels

What We Teach

  • Beginner
    • Yang-Style Tai Chi 16 Movement Hand Form
    • Simplified 24 Movement Form
  • Intermediate
    • Yang-Style Tai Chi 108 Movement Hand Form (Long Form)
    • Yang-Style Tai Chi Sword
    • Yang-Style Tai Chi Saber
  • Advanced
    • Yang-Style Tai Chi Completion Forms (Hand form, Sword, and Saber)

FAQ

  • Q: Do you teach Qigong or other internal martial arts?
  • A: No. We only teach Tai Chi. We do not teach Qigong or other martial arts.
